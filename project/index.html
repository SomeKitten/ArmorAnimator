Controls
    • Left click + drag to orbit camera
    • Right click + drag to look around FPS style (wasd, q, & e)
    • Middle click + drag to pan camera
    • Scroll to zoom in and out
    • Right/Left arrows to move along the timeline
    • Scroll when on top of a number to make it go up/down
    • x to center camera
    • Shift+space to type in the search
    • Escape to exit the search
    • Shift+drag to snap
    • Ctrl+drag to snap player heads
    • Ctrl+S to save your project as a .aaproj
    • Ctrl+E to export your project as a .zip (datapack)
